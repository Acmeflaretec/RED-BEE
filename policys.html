Refund Policy

We have a 7-day return policy, which means you have 7 days after receiving your item to request a return.

To be eligible for a return, your item must be in the same condition that you received it, unworn or unused, with tags, and in its original packaging. You’ll also need the receipt or proof of purchase.

To start a return, you can contact us at redbeehomeshop@gmail.com. Please note that returns will need to be sent to the following address:

[RED BEE Home & Health Essentials, 68/1520, 2nd Floor, C.M Mathew Brothers Arcade, Near Westway Hotel, Chakkorathukulam, Calicut - 673006

If your return is accepted, we’ll send you a return shipping label, as well as instructions on how and where to send your package. Items sent back to us without first requesting a return will not be accepted.

You can always contact us for any return question at redbeehomeshop@gmail.com.

Damages and issues

Please inspect your order upon reception and contact us immediately if the item is defective, damaged or if you receive the wrong item, so that we can evaluate the issue and make it right. Please make a 360-degree video while opening for clarifications.

Exchanges

The fastest way to ensure you get what you want is to return the item you have, and once the return is accepted, make a separate purchase for the new item.

Refunds

We will notify you once we’ve received and inspected your return, and let you know if the refund was approved or not. If approved, you’ll be automatically refunded on your original payment method within 10 business days.

If more than 15 business days have passed since we’ve approved your return, which is normally unlikely but may have delayed due to genuine reasons, then please contact us at redbeehomeshop@gmail.com. We shall do the needful.

Shipping Policy

RED BEE ships products across most parts of India. Orders are dispatched within 24 hours, excluding Sundays and National Holidays.

Shipping Charges: Shipping charges vary by location and apply to most orders. Free shipping is offered for orders above INR 500. These charges are non-refundable for returns or cancellations.

Changes in Shipping Charges: RED BEE reserves the right to modify shipping charges without prior notice. New charges will be reflected on product pages and during checkout.

Special Offers: Occasionally, RED BEE offers limited-time promotions with free shipping, subject to specific terms and conditions.

Estimated Delivery Time: For areas serviced by reputable couriers, delivery is typically within 3 to 4 business days after shipping, excluding Sundays and holidays. Larger items may require additional time. Delivery to other areas may take 5-7 days.

Unexpected Delays: While RED BEE aims to deliver promptly, unforeseen logistical challenges may cause delays. RED BEE is not liable for such delays and may cancel orders at its discretion if delivery exceeds usual timeframes.

Address Changes: Address changes are permitted for orders in confirmed or pending status before shipment. Variations in charges/taxes may affect address updates.

Hidden Charges: Prices are all-inclusive, with no additional charges during checkout.

Package Tracking: Customers receive an email with courier company details and tracking number. Tracking may take up to 48 hours to appear online.

Non-Availability on Delivery: Delivery partners attempt three deliveries before returning packages to the warehouse. Providing a mobile number facilitates faster delivery.
